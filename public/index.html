Emergency Help

Fast access to emergency services and life-saving guidance.

CPR (Adult)

  1. Check responsiveness & call emergency services.
  2. Place hands at center of chest. Push hard, ~2 inches deep.
  3. Compress at 100–120 per minute (to the beat of "Stayin' Alive").
  4. Continue until help arrives or person breathes.

Bleeding

  1. Apply firm pressure with clean cloth.
  2. Elevate the wound above heart level if possible.
  3. Do not remove embedded objects.
  4. Seek medical help for deep wounds.

Burns

  1. Cool burn under running water for 20 minutes.
  2. Remove tight clothing or jewelry near area.
  3. Cover loosely with sterile non-stick dressing.
  4. Do not apply ice, butter, or toothpaste.

Blood Donation

Healthy adults can donate every 56 days. Stay hydrated, eat iron-rich foods, and bring valid ID.
